Okonomiyaki Recipe

Ingredients

  • Okonomiyaki Flour Mix
  • Cabbage
  • Pork Belly
  • Eggs
  • Nagaimo
  • Tenkasu
  • Okonomiyaki Sauce
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Bonito Flakes
  • Green Onions
  • Seaweed

Directions

  1. Use batter Mix to create batter, and add chopped cabbage to the mix
  2. In a large pan coated in neutral oil, spread the batter in a circle on the pan to about a 2cm thickness
  3. Place 2-3 slices of pork belly on top of the Okonomiyaki
  4. Once the bottom of the pancake is brown, flip it over
  5. While cooking, press the pancake to fix its shape and keep it together. Cook for another 5 minutes
  6. Flip one last time and cook for another 2 minutes, then transfer to a plate.
  7. When serving, add toppings such as Okonomiyaki sauce, Kewpie mayo, etc.
